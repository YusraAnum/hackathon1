# AI-Native Textbook User Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive user guide for the AI-Native Textbook platform! This guide will help you navigate, understand, and make the most of our interactive learning environment for physical AI and humanoid robotics.

## About This Guide

The AI-Native Textbook platform provides an innovative approach to learning about physical AI and humanoid robotics. This user guide contains everything you need to know to get started, customize your experience, and overcome any challenges you might encounter.

## Getting Started

New to the platform? Start with our [Onboarding Guide](onboarding.md) to familiarize yourself with the interface and core features. You'll learn how to navigate the platform, customize your experience, and begin your learning journey.

### Translation Capabilities
Our platform supports multiple languages including English, Urdu, Spanish, French, German, and Chinese. Learn how to use the [Translation Features](translation-features.md) to access content in your preferred language.

### Personalization Options
Tailor your learning experience with our [Personalization Options](personalization.md). Adjust reading levels and learning styles to match your preferences and needs.

### Accessibility First
We're committed to inclusive design. Explore our [Accessibility Features](accessibility.md) to ensure the platform works for everyone, including users of assistive technologies.
