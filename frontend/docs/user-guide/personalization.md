# Personalization Options

The AI-Native Textbook platform offers extensive personalization options to tailor the learning experience to your individual needs and preferences.

## Enabling Personalization

1. Click the "Show Options" button in the "Optional Features" section
2. Check the "Enable Content Personalization" box
3. Select your preferred reading level and learning style
4. Click "Apply Settings" to activate personalization

## Reading Levels

Choose from three reading levels to match your current understanding:

### Beginner
- Simplified explanations with basic terminology
- Step-by-step breakdowns of complex concepts
- More examples and visual aids
- Focus on fundamental principles

### Intermediate
- Standard academic language with technical terms
- Balanced approach between concepts and applications
- Moderate complexity in examples
- Assumes basic understanding of prerequisites

### Advanced
- Technical language and complex terminology
- Focus on advanced applications and theory
- Challenging examples and exercises
- Assumes strong foundational knowledge

## Learning Styles

Select the learning style that best matches your preferences:

### Visual
- Enhanced visual elements and diagrams
- Color-coded concepts and relationships
- Infographics and visual summaries
- Emphasis on charts and graphical representations

### Auditory
- Text-to-speech capabilities (when available)
- Emphasis on verbal explanations
- Audio-enhanced examples
- Focus on discussions and explanations

### Reading/Writing
- Detailed written explanations
- Emphasis on reading comprehension
- Written exercises and activities
- Text-based examples and summaries

### Kinesthetic
- Interactive elements and hands-on activities
- Practical applications and experiments
- Movement-based learning suggestions
- Focus on doing and experiencing concepts

## Personalization Benefits

- **Adaptive Content**: Textbook content adjusts to your selected preferences
- **Customized Examples**: Examples are tailored to match your learning style
- **Optimized Complexity**: Content complexity matches your reading level
- **Enhanced Engagement**: Personalized approach increases learning effectiveness

## Saving Preferences

Your personalization settings are saved in your browser's local storage, so they will persist between visits. You can reset to default settings using the "Reset to Default" button.

## Accessibility and Personalization

Personalization features are designed to work seamlessly with accessibility features. Screen readers and other assistive technologies will properly interpret personalized content.

## Tips for Best Results

1. Start with intermediate settings if you're unsure of your level
2. Experiment with different learning styles to discover what works best
3. Adjust settings as your understanding grows
4. Combine personalization with translation features for optimal learning
