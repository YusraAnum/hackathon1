# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is the AI-Native Textbook?
The AI-Native Textbook is an interactive learning platform that combines physical AI and humanoid robotics education. It provides comprehensive content with real-time translation, personalization options, and accessibility features to create an inclusive learning environment.

### Who is this platform designed for?
This platform is designed for students, researchers, and professionals interested in physical AI and humanoid robotics. It caters to different learning levels from beginner to advanced.

### Is the platform free to use?
The platform offers both free and premium content. Basic access to core textbook materials is free, with additional features available through premium subscriptions.

## Translation Features

### What languages are supported?
Currently, we support English, Urdu, Spanish, French, German, and Chinese. We're continuously working to add more languages.

### How accurate are the translations?
Our translations use advanced AI models and are regularly reviewed for accuracy. While highly accurate, technical terminology may occasionally vary between languages.

### Can I switch languages while reading?
Yes, you can switch between languages at any time. Your language preference will be saved for future visits.

## Personalization

### How do personalization settings work?
Personalization adapts the content complexity and presentation style to match your reading level and learning preferences. You can adjust these settings in the "Optional Features" section.

### Will my personalization settings affect the content quality?
Personalization changes how content is presented, not the underlying information. All versions maintain the same educational value and accuracy.

### Can I combine personalization with translation?
Yes, you can use both translation and personalization features simultaneously for the best learning experience.

## Accessibility

### Is the platform accessible to users with disabilities?
Yes, the platform follows WCAG 2.1 AA standards and includes features like keyboard navigation, screen reader support, and customizable text presentation.

### Can I use assistive technologies with this platform?
The platform is compatible with most assistive technologies including screen readers, keyboard navigation tools, and voice recognition software.

### How do I report accessibility issues?
Use the feedback system to report any accessibility barriers you encounter. We're committed to continuously improving accessibility.

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?
We support modern versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, use the latest version of your preferred browser.

### Do I need to create an account?
Account creation is optional for basic access to textbook content. Some advanced features may require registration.

### Can I access the content offline?
Currently, the platform requires an internet connection. We're exploring offline access options for future releases.

### How is my data used?
We use your preferences to personalize your learning experience. We do not share personal data with third parties. See our privacy policy for more details.

## Content Questions

### How often is content updated?
Content is regularly reviewed and updated to reflect the latest developments in physical AI and humanoid robotics. Major updates occur quarterly.

### Can I suggest new topics or improvements?
Yes, we welcome feedback and suggestions through our feedback system. Community input helps shape the platform's development.

### Are there practice exercises or assessments?
The platform includes interactive examples and exercises throughout the content. More comprehensive assessment tools are in development.

## Support

### How do I get help if I encounter issues?
Check our [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md) section first. If you need further assistance, use the feedback system or contact our support team.

### Where can I find additional resources?
Additional resources and references are provided throughout the textbook content. Check the end of each chapter for recommended readings and links.

### Can I provide feedback on the content?
Yes, we encourage user feedback to improve the platform. Use the feedback system to share your thoughts and suggestions.

## Privacy and Security

### Is my information secure?
Yes, we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data transmission is encrypted.

### Do you track my learning progress?
We only track information necessary to provide personalized experiences and improve the platform. You can control what data is collected in your settings.

### Can I delete my data?
Yes, you can request deletion of your personal data through our privacy controls or by contacting support.
